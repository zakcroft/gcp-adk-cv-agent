Senior Backend Engineer
InnovateTech Solutions | London, UK | Full-time

About InnovateTech:
We're a fast-growing fintech company revolutionizing digital payments. Our platform processes millions of transactions daily, and we're looking for talented engineers to help us scale.

Role Overview:
We're seeking an experienced Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform Engineering team. You'll be responsible for designing and building highly scalable, reliable backend systems that power our payment infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities:
• Design and implement robust, scalable microservices architecture
• Build high-performance RESTful APIs handling millions of requests per day
• Optimize database queries and system performance
• Lead technical design discussions and mentor junior engineers
• Ensure system reliability, security, and compliance with financial regulations
• Collaborate with product managers and frontend engineers to deliver features
• Participate in on-call rotation for production support

Required Skills & Experience:
• 5+ years of professional backend development experience
• Expert-level proficiency in Python (Django or FastAPI preferred)
• Strong experience with microservices architecture and distributed systems
• Deep understanding of database design and optimization (PostgreSQL, Redis)
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
• Proven track record of building and scaling high-traffic systems
• Strong knowledge of API design principles and best practices
• Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
• Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills
• Strong communication skills and ability to work in cross-functional teams

Nice to Have:
• Experience in fintech or payment processing systems
• Knowledge of event-driven architectures (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
• Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana)
• Contributions to open-source projects
• Experience with CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code
